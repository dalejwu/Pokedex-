About Pokemon

Pokemon are creatures of all shapes and sizes who live in the wild or alongside humans. During their adventures, Pokemon grow and become stronger, sometimes even evolving into different Pokemon.

Pokemon Origins

Pokemon was created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1996. The franchise began as a pair of video games for the original Game Boy, developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo.

The idea for Pokemon came from Tajiri's childhood hobby of insect collecting, a popular pastime in Japan. He wanted to create a game that could share the excitement of catching and collecting creatures with a new generation.
